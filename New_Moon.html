  • Genre: Fantasy/Romance

Book cover:

New Moon continues the story of Bella Swan and her complicated life in Forks, Washington. At the start of the book, Bella is adjusting to her relationship with Edward Cullen, a vampire. However, during Bella's 18th birthday party, Edward accidentally puts her in danger. Fearing for her life, Edward decides to leave Forks with his family, believing that Bella will be safer without him. Devastated by Edward's departure, Bella falls into a deep depression and struggles to find purpose. Over time, she develops a close friendship with Jacob Black, a kind and loyal childhood friend. Bella soon learns that Jacob and his pack are werewolves, sworn enemies of vampires. Despite this, her bond with Jacob strengthens, giving her emotional support as she tries to cope with Edward's absence.

Meanwhile, Bella learns that Edward, believing Bella is dead, is planning to provoke the Volturi, the vampire ruling coven, into killing him. Determined to save him, Bella makes a dangerous journey to Italy, risking her life to prevent Edward from making a fatal mistake. Throughout New Moon, Bella confronts themes of love, loss, and identity, experiencing the pain of heartbreak while exploring new relationships and the dangers of her supernatural world. The story ends with Bella realizing the depth of her feelings for Edward and setting the stage for the conflicts and choices she will face in Eclipse.
