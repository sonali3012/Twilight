Twilight follows Bella Swan, a 17-year-old girl who moves from sunny Arizona to the rainy town of Forks, Washington, to live with her father, Charlie. At her new high school, Bella becomes intrigued by the mysterious Edward Cullen, a handsome but distant classmate who seems to possess strange abilities.Bella soon discovers that Edward and his family are vampires—creatures who survive on animal blood rather than human. Despite the danger, Bella and Edward fall deeply in love. Their relationship grows complicated as Edward struggles between his love for Bella and his instinct to protect her from his world.
