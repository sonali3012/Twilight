Book Information

  • Author: Stephenie Meyer
  • Genre: Fantasy/Romance
  • Published: August 7th, 2007

Book cover:

Eclipse picks up with Bella Swan continuing her life in Forks, Washington, as she faces increasingly complex challenges in her relationships and supernatural surroundings. Bella is deeply in love with Edward Cullen, but her bond with Jacob Black, her childhood friend and a werewolf, grows stronger, creating a painful love triangle. She struggles to balance her intense feelings for both Edward and Jacob while confronting the difficult choices her heart and future demand. Meanwhile, a dark threat emerges in the form of Victoria, a vampire seeking revenge for the death of her mate, James. She raises an army of newborn vampires with the sole purpose of killing Bella. This looming danger forces the Cullens and the Quileute werewolves to put aside their longstanding rivalry and work together to protect Bella and their community.

Throughout the story, Bella becomes more aware of the consequences of her decisions, especially regarding her potential transformation into a vampire. She is torn between remaining human, which allows her to maintain her friendship with Jacob, and becoming immortal, which secures her life with Edward forever. Bella's growth is evident as she navigates the risks of the supernatural world, the moral complexities of her relationships, and the danger that constantly surrounds her. By the end of Eclipse, Bella has survived the vampire threat, deepened her understanding of her feelings for both Edward and Jacob, and set the stage for the dramatic events of the final book, Breaking Dawn.
